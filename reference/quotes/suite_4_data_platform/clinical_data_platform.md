## REDCap vs Commercial EDC Comparison

| Feature | REDCap | Medidata Rave | Veeva Vault |
|---------|--------|---------------|-------------|
| Annual cost | $50,000 | $200,000+ | $150,000+ |
| 21 CFR Part 11 | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Ease of use | Excellent | Good | Good |
| Customization | High | Medium | Medium |
| Local hosting | Yes | No (SaaS) | No (SaaS) |
| Canadian data residency | Yes (UAlberta) | Requires negotiation | Requires negotiation |

### Why REDCap for CTAP

1. **Cost-effective:** Significantly lower than commercial alternatives
2. **Data sovereignty:** Can be hosted on-premises at UAlberta
3. **Flexibility:** Highly customizable for diverse trial types
4. **Integration:** Strong API for LIMS, EMR integration
5. **Academic standard:** Widely used in Canadian research institutions
6. **Compliance:** 21 CFR Part 11 capable with proper configuration
