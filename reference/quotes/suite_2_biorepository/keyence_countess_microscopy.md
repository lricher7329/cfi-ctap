**Manufacturer:** Keyence Corporation
**Website:** [keyence.com/bz-x800](https://www.keyence.com/ss/products/microscope/bz-x800/)

#### Key Features
- Inverted, fully-motorized widefield microscope
- Fluorescence, brightfield, and phase-contrast imaging
- Live cell analysis capability
- Well plate and multi-slide screening
- Automated cell quantification
- Compact benchtop design

- Fluorescence: Up to 6 channels
- Camera: High-sensitivity cooled CCD
- Software: BZ-X800 Analyzer with AI-assisted analysis
