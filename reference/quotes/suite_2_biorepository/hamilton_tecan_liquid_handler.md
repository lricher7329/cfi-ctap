## Budget Justification

The $650,000 CAD budget aligns with a fully configured Hamilton STAR or Tecan Fluent 1080 system with:
- Multiple pipetting heads (8-channel, 96-channel, 384-channel)
- Robotic gripper for plate/tube handling
- Integrated barcode reader
- Tube decapping/recapping module
- Integration with OpenSpecimen LIMS
- Installation, validation, and training

### Recommended Configuration for CTAP Biorepository
- **Primary:** Hamilton Microlab STAR with Span-8, CO-RE 96, iSWAP gripper
- **Integrated modules:** Barcode reader, tube decapper, plate hotel
- **Software:** Venus software with LIMS integration module
