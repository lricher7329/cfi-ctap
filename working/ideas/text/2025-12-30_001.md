# Idea

Integrate the TRE value proposition from the research document throughout the project, transforming the narrative from "technical infrastructure" to "proven clinical impact infrastructure."

## Context

The research document `ideas/research/tre_value_proposition.md` contains compelling evidence for TRE value:

1. **GEMINI/Chartwatch**: 26% reduction in unexpected deaths - operational proof that integrated data architecture saves lives
2. **Learning Health System**: Closed-loop model where data → research → clinical action → outcomes
3. **Unstructured Data**: 80% of healthcare data is in clinical notes; AWS Bedrock/SageMaker enables NLP
4. **VITAL Federation**: Pan-Canadian network enabling rare disease research with sufficient statistical power
5. **DHDP Partnership**: 97 consortium partners for federated learning without data movement
